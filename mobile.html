How it works

Workplace transformation doesn't happen in silos.

Most CRE teams run transaction management, lease administration, and portfolio planning across disconnected systems. LiquidSpace connects them all.

Access
Book workspace anywhere, within policy.
Every booking is tied to your eligibility rules, budgets, and approval workflows — generating the behavioral signal that powers every decision downstream.
Operations
Govern programs. Manage commitments.
From setting who can book and where, to managing every license and transaction — all in one system. AI supports every step so you focus on decisions, not admin.
Strategy
Know what your portfolio should do next.
Hub placement, footprint sizing, lease renewal — modeled with live behavioral data from your own portfolio. So when it's time to decide, you already know the answer.
Each layer makes the next one smarter.
Access generates signal → Operations governs action → Strategy refines decisions ↻
